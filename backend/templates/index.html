Slug-Practice Help Documentation

Overview

Slug-Practice is an innovative tool designed to automatically create practice problems to aid in academic study. By following a few simple steps, users can generate custom flashcards tailored to specific subjects and difficulty levels.

Getting Started

Step 1: Enter Keywords

Start by inputting a valid academic keyword along with a related example into the designated fields. This will ensure that the generated problems are relevant to the desired subject area.

Step 2: Choose Difficulty and Format

Select your preferred difficulty level from the following options:

  • Easy
  • Medium
  • Hard

Then, choose the format of the question you would like to generate:

  • Simple (Open-ended question)
  • Multiple Choice
  • True or False

Step 3: Generate Flashcards

Click the 'Generate' button to initiate the creation of your practice problems. The Language Learning Model (LLM) will process your request, which typically takes approximately 15 seconds.

Step 4: Review Flashcards

Upon generation, the first flashcard will appear on your screen. Use the navigation buttons to move between the flashcards in your set. To view the answer to a question, simply click on the flashcard.

Step 5: Return to Front Page

If you wish to generate a new set of flashcards or modify your selections, click the 'Home' link to return to the main page.

Disclaimer

While Slug-Practice's LLM strives for high accuracy in the answers provided, it is important to verify the correctness of the content. The answers generated are based on the data available to the LLM during training, and as such, they may not always be perfect. Users are encouraged to exercise caution and double-check the answers for accuracy.

For any further assistance or feedback, please contact our support team.
